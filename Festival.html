1. Pongal is a four-day harvest festival celebrated in Tamil Nadu, marking the end of the winter solstice.
  2. It is dedicated to the Sun God and involves cooking a special dish called 'Pongal' made from newly harvested rice, milk, and jaggery.
  3. The festival includes rituals, traditional music, dance, and colorful kolams (rangoli) drawn at doorsteps.
  4. Pongal symbolizes gratitude for the harvest and is a time for family gatherings and community celebrations.
  1. The Chennai Dance Festival is an annual event celebrating classical dance forms like Bharatanatyam, Kathak, and Odissi.
  2. It showcases performances by renowned dancers and emerging talents from across India.
  3. The festival is held at the historic Kalakshetra Foundation, a cultural institution dedicated to the preservation of Indian arts.
  4. It attracts dance enthusiasts and tourists, promoting Chennai as a cultural hub.
